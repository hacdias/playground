## Rolle's Theorem

If $f$ is:

- continuous in $[a, b]$,
- differentiable in $]a, b[$,
- $f(a) = f(b)$
- **then**, $\exists c \in ]a, b[: f'(c) = 0$ 

## Bolzano's Theorem

If $f$ is:

- continuous in the limited and closed $[a, b]$ interval,
- $f(a) \neq f(b)$,
- **then**, $\exists c \in ]a,b[: f(c) = k$
